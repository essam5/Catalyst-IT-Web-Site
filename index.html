Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our software development services

15+ Questions Answered

What types of software does Catalyst IT build?

We build custom web applications, mobile apps (Android and iOS), desktop software, backend APIs, cloud solutions, and enterprise systems. We also handle legacy modernization, system integration, and ongoing maintenance.

Which technologies and programming languages do you use?

We work with Python, Django, FastAPI, Node.js, Java, Spring Boot, .NET, PHP, Laravel, React, Angular, Vue, Flutter, React Native, Kotlin, Swift, AWS, Azure, Docker, Kubernetes, PostgreSQL, MySQL, and MongoDB — selecting the best stack for each project.

How long does a typical project take?

Timelines depend on scope and complexity. An MVP may take 6–10 weeks, while larger enterprise systems can span several months. We provide a detailed timeline during the discovery phase before development begins.

Do you provide support after the project is launched?

Yes. We offer ongoing software maintenance, performance optimization, bug fixes, and feature enhancements. Many clients work with us on a long-term retainer for continuous development and support.

How do you communicate during a project?

We maintain transparent communication through regular sprint updates, progress demos, and direct access to your project team. You'll always know the status of your project and what's coming next.

How does project pricing work?

We offer project-based quotes for defined scopes, monthly retainers for dedicated teams, and custom enterprise agreements. Pricing is based on scope, complexity, and timeline — we provide a detailed proposal after understanding your requirements.

What is included in a dedicated team retainer?

A dedicated team retainer includes assigned engineers, agile sprint delivery, priority support, technical consulting, and ongoing maintenance. Team size and hours can be adjusted as your needs evolve.

Do you require an upfront payment?

Project-based engagements typically follow milestone-based payments tied to deliverables. Retainer agreements are billed monthly. Specific terms are outlined in your project proposal.

Can I start with a small project before committing to a larger engagement?

Absolutely. Many clients begin with an MVP or a specific module to evaluate our work before expanding the engagement. We're happy to start small and grow the partnership over time.

Do you handle cloud deployment and DevOps?

Yes. We set up CI/CD pipelines, containerize applications with Docker, orchestrate with Kubernetes, and deploy to AWS or Azure. We also configure monitoring, logging, and auto-scaling for production environments.

Can you integrate with our existing systems and APIs?

System integration is one of our core services. We connect new software with third-party APIs, payment gateways, CRMs, ERPs, and legacy systems while ensuring data consistency and reliability.

Who owns the source code after the project?

You do. Upon project completion and final payment, all source code, documentation, and related assets are transferred to you. We believe clients should have full ownership of what we build together.

How do you ensure code quality?

We follow code review practices, write automated tests, use linting tools, and adhere to established architecture patterns. Our goal is code that is readable, testable, and easy for your team to maintain.

Can you modernize our legacy software?

Yes. We assess existing systems, plan incremental migrations, refactor outdated code, and rebuild components on modern stacks — minimizing disruption to your daily operations throughout the process.

Do you build AI and automation features?

We integrate AI capabilities and workflow automation into existing products — including intelligent document processing, chatbots, and automated business logic — tailored to your specific use case and data.
